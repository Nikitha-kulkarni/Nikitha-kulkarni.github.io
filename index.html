Tom And Jerry

Tom and Jerry is considered as one of the best cartoon in the world and has too much of fan following especially of the 90’s kids. Tom and Jerry are two different characters of the cartoon. Tom is a cat and Jerry is a mouse. Jerry is always in trouble of Tom but in the end, Jerry creates lots of troublesome situation to Tom cat.

Oggy and the cockroaches

Despite his lazy nature, Oggy is a diplomatic and easy-natured cat. He often displays kindness toward animals and often takes care of them. Indeed, he could not bring himself to kill an octopus in one episode.

Chhota Bheem

Chhota Bheem is an Indian animated series adventures about a boy named Bheem and his friends in fictional village of Dholakpur. Bheem and his friends are usually involved in protecting the village from various evil forces.

Mr bean

Mr. Bean is immature, self-absorbed, extremely competitive and brings various abnormal schemes and contrivances to everyday tasks. He rarely speaks, and when he does, it is normally only a few mumbled words which are in a comically low-pitched voice.

Noddy

Noddy is a young and eager boy living in Toyland. Each episode of revolves around Noddy having a nice party with all of his close friends. Even though Noddy is little, he has a BIG heart and a lot to give his friends.

Doraemon

Doraemon is a robotic cat that comes from the 22nd century. He comes to 20th century and stays with Nobi Family. The Nobis love Doraemon very much. So Doraemon always help the Nobis with the devices from 22nd century.

Roll no. 21

Roll no. 21 is an indian animated television series. It is a modern take on Krishna and Kansa's rivalry. In this show Kansa has reincarnated as Kanishk sir, the principal of Mathura anath ashram where he makes evil plans to take over the world. Krishna has reincarnated as Kris, a student at Kanishk's school.

Mickey Mouse

Mickey Mouse is one of the most popular cartoon characters for kids. Each one of us has seen the shows and read the stories related to Mickey Mouse. Mickey Mouse had many friends, but among them all was one special friend close to his heart- ‘Minnie Mouse’.

Mighty Raju

Mighty Raju is the story of a small boy named Raju, who lives with his parents and his dog Moby in the city of Arya nagar. Raju's father, Swami is a very famous scientist.

Thomas & Friends

The Thomas the Tank Engine stories were conceived by a young British boy early in the 20th century. Thomas is a blue tank engine who lives on the Island of Sodor. He is engine No. 1 on the North Western Railway and works on the Main Line, but helps out on Edward's Branch Line. He is the Main Protagonist.

Oswald

Oswald - The protagonist of the series, a blue octopus who lives in an apartment complex with his pet Weenie. He is gentle, polite, and kindhearted; Oswald will do anything to help his friends. His favorite hobby is playing the piano. Nearly everyone is an anthropomorphic object or creature, and many of the buildings and vehicles are shaped like everyday objects.

Dora the explorer

Dora is a true heroine–an Indiana Jones for the preschool set. She is a born explorer, always eager for the next adventure. Though she’s only seven, she serves as something of a big sister to her best friend, Boots, and to the viewer as well.
